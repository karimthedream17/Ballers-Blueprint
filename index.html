About Us

Ballers Blueprint is a mission-driven platform built to guide student athletes in Grades 8–12 through their post-secondary or professional soccer journey. Whether it's college, U SPORTS, NCAA, or a pro trial overseas, we help you understand your options and make the best decision for your future.

What We Offer

  • ✅ Free guides, blog posts & video resources
  • ✅ Optional private consulting calls
  • ✅ Team & recruiter connections for qualified players
  • ✅ TikTok/Instagram content breaking down real pathways
  • ✅ Brand/club partnerships that support you, not sell to you
